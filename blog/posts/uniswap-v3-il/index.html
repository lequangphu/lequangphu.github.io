Introduction

Liquidity Providers (LPs) deposit assets into Uniswap’s pools to earn trading fee paid by traders. It’s an investing strategy. Let’s call the strategy LPing.

In the period of 60 days from July 1 to August 29, 2023, Uniswap V3 TVL on Ethereum ranged between $2.3B and $2.9B. TVL was trending down from $2.8B to $2.4B.

What can they do with the assets instead? The simplest stategy is to hold them, let’s call the stragtegy Hodling.

Compare to Hodling, the upside of LPing is earning fee. What is the downside? It’s the loss of assets value, i.e. impermanent loss (IL).

Uniswap document has a section to understand more about IL, which you can read here.

In this post, let’s explore IL of LPing in Uniswap V3.

Impermanent Loss

Uniswap V3

During the 60 days period, on average, IL of Uniswap V3 ranged between $59K and $1.1M per day. IL percentage ranged between 0.002% and 0.042% per day.

Top Pools

Average daily IL of top 8 TVL pools ranged between $1 (USDC-USDT : 1%, top stablecoins pool) and $16K (USDC-WETH : 0.05%, the largest TVL pool). % IL of most of the pools is either 0.001% or 0.004%.

The top TVL pools are also the top volume pools.

Average daily IL of top IL pools ranged between $21K (TOWN-WETH : 1%) and $277K (ITP-WETH : 0.3%). Meanwhile, % IL ranged between 1.6% (FUN-WETH : 1%) and 71.4% (USDC-UST : 1%). Of 8 top pools, % IL of 3 pools are 20% or more, % IL of other 5 pools are 5% or less.

Top LP

Interestingly, 7 out of 8 in top 8 positions (largest TVL) didn’t have IL. IL of the largest position was only $1.1K, i.e. 0.003% in percentage of TVL.
